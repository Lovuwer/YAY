What the customer does

Snap. Match. Checkout.

No typing. No app fatigue. No building carts line by line — just the same paper list they'd have handed the counter clerk.

Matches against the exact SKUs in your ERPNext item master — not a generic national catalog. If your store carries Double Horse toor dal, that's what goes in the cart. Not Tata Sampann.

Bilingual OCR

Malayalam, English, or a hybrid scribble — handled by a vision model trained on Kerala retail handwriting.

Store-local matching

Only SKUs physically present in the nearest Freshlife store are proposed. No substitutions you didn't authorize.

One-tap checkout

The matched cart drops straight into your existing checkout flow — pickup, home delivery, or scheduled.
